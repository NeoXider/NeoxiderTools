# RpgTargetSelector

**What it is:** a reusable `MonoBehaviour` target selector for AI, skill logic, and spell casting.

**Navigation:** [← RPG](./README.md)

---

## Supports

- `Nearest`
- `Farthest`
- `LowestCurrentHp`
- `HighestCurrentHp`
- `LowestHpPercent`
- `HighestLevel`
- `Random`
